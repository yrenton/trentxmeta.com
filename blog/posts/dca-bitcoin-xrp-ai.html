DCA, AI and Marketing

This post explains how I approach three core areas of my work.

DCA into Bitcoin & XRP: I buy these assets regularly, regardless of short‑term price swings. It’s a disciplined, long‑term plan.

AI: I use artificial intelligence tools to automate tasks, analyze data and build products that save time and increase output. AI is a toolbox, not a buzzword.

Marketing: I develop marketing strategies, create funnels and launch campaigns. Marketing is about connecting the right message with the right audience.

These three areas complement each other, but they are separate disciplines. By keeping them distinct, I avoid confusion and can focus on building value in each.
