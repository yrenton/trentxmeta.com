DCA into Bitcoin & XRP, AI and the Future

Posted on September 18, 2025

When it comes to investing, my approach has always been long-term and steady. Dollar-cost averaging (DCA) into Bitcoin and XRP has given me peace of mind in volatile markets. Instead of trying to time the perfect entry, I contribute small amounts regularly—building a position over time and benefiting from the market’s natural ups and downs.

In parallel, I’m fascinated by the ways artificial intelligence can amplify projects and communities. From automating content creation to optimizing marketing efforts, AI has become a critical tool in my toolkit. With Doginal Dogs and other initiatives, leveraging AI tools means I can spend more time on strategy and community-building and less on repetitive tasks.

Looking forward, I believe we’re still in the early stages of what crypto and AI can achieve together. Blockchain’s transparency and decentralization paired with AI’s ability to learn and adapt has the potential to transform industries—from finance to art. I’m bullish on the future because I see both technologies creating stronger, fairer and more creative digital economies.

None of this is financial advice; it’s simply my strategy and view of where we’re headed. Always do your own research and decide what makes sense for your risk tolerance and goals. Thanks for reading!
